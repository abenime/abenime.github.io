Project Overview

HuFeed is a comprehensive student-organization networking and event management platform designed to bridge the gap between students and organizations on campuses. Built with React Native and Firebase, this mobile-first application provides a seamless experience for discovering events, connecting with like-minded individuals, and building communities.

The platform revolutionizes how students discover and engage with campus events, allowing organizations to reach their target audience effectively and students to stay informed about opportunities that matter to them.

Key Features

  • Event Discovery: Browse and filter events by category, date, and location with smart recommendations
  • User Profiles: Create detailed profiles with interests, skills, and social connections
  • Real-time Notifications: Stay updated with instant push notifications for events and interactions
  • Event Management: Organizations can create, manage, and promote events directly from the app
  • Social Network: Connect with other students, follow organizations, and build your campus network
  • RSVP System: Seamless event registration and attendance tracking
